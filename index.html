FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Explore our frequently asked questions to learn more about Neuros’s features, security, integration capabilities, and more

What is Neuros and how does it differ from other analytics platforms?

Neuros is an AI-powered business analytics platform designed to provide real-time insights, predictive analytics, and seamless integrations with various tools. Unlike traditional analytics platforms, Neuros harnesses the power of artificial intelligence to offer more accurate forecasts, customizable dashboards, and a user-friendly interface tailored for businesses of all sizes.

How does the AI-driven predictive analytics feature work?

Neuros analyzes your historical and real-time business data, finds patterns, and uses AI models to forecast possible outcomes. This helps teams understand demand, revenue changes, customer behavior, and operational risks before they happen.

Is my data secure with Neuros?

Yes. Neuros is built with strong security practices, including encrypted data handling, controlled access, and privacy-focused workflows. Your business data stays protected while your team works with insights across dashboards and reports.

Can I integrate Neuros with other tools and platforms I currently use?

Neuros can connect with common business tools such as CRMs, ERPs, marketing platforms, spreadsheets, and reporting systems. These integrations help keep your analytics in one place without changing your whole workflow.

How is the pricing structured for Neuros? Are there any hidden fees?

Pricing is structured around the needs of your team, including usage, features, and scale. Neuros keeps pricing clear so you know what is included before you start, without surprise charges added later.

I’m new to business analytics. Does Neuros offer any support or tutorials?

Yes. Neuros includes helpful onboarding resources, tutorials, and support options for new users. Teams can learn how to build dashboards, read insights, and use predictive analytics step by step.
